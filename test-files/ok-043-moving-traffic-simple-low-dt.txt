Test input file for Hobyah, a 1D tunnel vent program.
July 2024
A file that verifies the development of road traffic.  Has three tunnels
tunnel and three types of traffic.
In the first tunnel the traffic moves at constant speed and
fills the tunnel end to end.
In the other two tunnels the traffic is a block of stationary traffic
80 m long (the length of two cells in the MoC calculation).  Other
losses (portal losses and friction) are minimised.
In the second tunnel the block of stationary traffic starts and stops
at gridpoints.
In the third tunnel the block of stationary traffic starts and stops
halfway between gridpoints.
The results of the second and third tunnel should be the same; if they
aren't something is wrong with the logic used to calculate the traffic
drag.

The calculation excludes the block correction term.

begin settings
  version 1
  runtype calc
  frictiontype Darcy
  QA1 2
  QA2 Software testing
  QA3 Files that work and test different capabilities
  aero_step 0.05
  aero_time 400.
end settings

begin sectypes
  C+C1    103   55   -0.036
  C+C2    103   55   -0.00000001
end sectypes

begin tunnel first
  back 10000 portal  0  C+C1
  fwd  13000 portal  0
end tunnel

begin tunnel second
  back   0 portal  20  C+C2   zeta_in := 0  zeta_out := 0
  fwd  200 portal -20   zeta_in := 0  zeta_out := 0
end tunnel

begin tunnel third
  back   0 portal  20  C+C2   zeta_in := 0  zeta_out := 0
  fwd  200 portal -20   zeta_in := 0  zeta_out := 0
end tunnel

begin traffictypes
  calculate without blockage correction term
# keyword   name   area  C_d  PCU/veh
  vehicle   car    2.0   0.4    1
  vehicle   LCV    4.0   0.9    1
  vehicle   HGV    6.0   1.0    3   10   2
end traffictypes

begin route one
  origin 9000
  portal 10000
  begin tunnels
    first
  end tunnels
end route

begin route two
  origin 9000
  portal 10000
  begin tunnels
    second
  end tunnels
  begin lanes
     2    15000
  end lanes
end route

begin route three
  origin 9000
  portal 10000
  begin tunnels
    third
  end tunnels
  begin lanes
     2    15000
  end lanes
end route

begin trafficsteady both
