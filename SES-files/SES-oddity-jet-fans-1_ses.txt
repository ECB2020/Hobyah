                         SES v4.1 file for illustrating undocumented behaviour of jet fans.
                         A contrived example for a set of notes on some oddities in SES.
                         Jet fans are located at the back end of the segment and experience
                         the conditions in the annulus around any trains crossing the back
                         end boundary.
                         In this file the trains do not cross boundaries and the airflow
                         in the tunnels is symmetric.
